Old World — Family Combo Theory

Thinking in the multiplayer context.

For each nation: which family to make your capital, which to keep as a military seat, your third family, and a family worth having the most cities with. These are mostly my own biases — I like a science cap and a military seat, and I'll spam the families I think are worth it (keeping the rest within ~3 cities, e.g. 1 Artisan to 4 Statesmen). You'll break all of this constantly; they're not rules, just gentle guidelines — hopefully a handy way to think through your city / family / nation setups.
