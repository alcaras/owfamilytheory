The Family Theory

Why each family lands where it does — which make good capitals, which make good military seats, and which are worth spamming. Multiplayer-flavoured, and all just my own biases.

Every game you're really doing one thing: slotting a nation's families into a handful of jobs. I want one capital (ideally science), one military seat I keep small for units & generals, and one – two spam families I grow as wide as I can. Everything else I keep within ~3 cities of the pack (e.g. 1 Artisan to 4 Statesmen). A family is only "good" relative to the role you're asking it to play.
Capitalone tall science / resource city Mil seatkept small — units, XP, generals Spamgrow it widest
None of this is a rule. You'll break all of it constantly — a great resource start, a Wonder, a UU, a war that hands you a free city. These are just the defaults I fall back to when nothing on the map argues otherwise. Whatever I'm not capping or spamming, I keep within roughly three cities of the leader so no single family runs away from the rest.
